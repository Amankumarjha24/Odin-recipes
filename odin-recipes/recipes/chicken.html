🌿 Recipe: Spicy Garlic Butter Chicke

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken breast (cut into bite-sized pices)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbps olive oil
  • 6-7 garlic cloves
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 capsicum bell pepper
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp paprika (optional for smoky flavor)
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbps soy sauce
  • Fresh coriander or parsley (for garnish)

Cooking steps

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in pan.
  2. Add garlic and sute until golden brown.
  3. Toss in onions and capsicum, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add chicken pieces, cook on medium flame until they turn golden.
  5. Sprinkle chili flakes black pepper, paprika, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Pour in soya sause, stir until chicken is coated and cooked through
  7. Granish with fresh coriander/parsley and serve hot.

👉 Best enjoyed with butter naan, fried rice, or even as a filling in wraps
