🍫 Recipe: Chocolate Lava Cake

🛒 Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate (chopped)
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • A pinch of salt
  • Butter & flour (for greasing ramekins)

👩‍🍳 Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). Grease ramekins with butter and dust lightly with flour.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together (double boiler or microwave). Stir until smooth.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, yolks, sugar, and vanilla until pale and fluffy.
  4. Gently fold in melted chocolate mixture.
  5. Sift flour and salt, then fold into the batter.
  6. Pour batter into ramekins (¾ full).
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes until edges are firm but center is soft.
  8. Carefully invert onto a plate, dust with powdered sugar, and serve warm with ice cream or berries.
